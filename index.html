General Interests

Job Seeking and More About Me

I am a recent graduate in search of a new symbiotic job environment. Ideally, a situation in which I am able to provide an employer with a base package of skills and knowledge, while also being an elastic worker with the potential of being molded to best fit my employer. In return, I am looking for the opportunity to learn new skills and hopefully have the tutelage of my superiors to best implement these skills into my toolbox.

My brain tends to be very analytic and systematic. I enjoy probabilistic thinking, predictive analysis, and working in the equilibrium between efficiency and perfection in order to maximize surplus value in my work. That said, I have somewhat of an obsessive brain and do not like to leave problems unsettled. Therefore, I enjoy finding unique and creative solutions to such problems, either by working around them, or by changing my frame of reference.
