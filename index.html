General Interests

In a broad sense, I am interested in the application of data to encourage better decisions. In other words, I am interested in using statistics, data science, and domain specific knowledge to create systematic and data informed decision making processes. To this point, the bulk of my experience has been in the field of professional sports, but these tools are relevant to nearly every field.

More About Me

My brain tends to be very analytic and systematic. I enjoy probabilistic thinking, predictive analysis, and working in the equilibrium between efficiency and perfection in order to maximize surplus value in my work. That said, I have somewhat of an obsessive brain and do not like to leave problems unsettled. Therefore, I enjoy finding unique and creative solutions to such problems, either by working around them, or by changing my frame of reference.
