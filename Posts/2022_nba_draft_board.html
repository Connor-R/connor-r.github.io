2022 NBA Draft Board

June 23, 2022

Introduction

A proper intro-type blurb will be somehwere in my 2018, 2019, 2020, 2016-2020 draft retrospective, or 2021 posts. I don't feel like copying it here; these posts are mostly for posterity. As per last year, the draft snuck up on me so this was a bit rushed (please excuse any spelling/grammatical errors, I didn't do a lot of editing), so I didn't go quite as deep as I have in the past. Any players I looked at but didn't make my tiers (or I didn't have time to take a deeper look at) are noted in an Honorable Mention tier, sorted by an aggregated ranking method I used to find relevant prospects.

Tier 1 (~60+ FV)

  1. Paolo Banchero - PF - Duke
    • Grade: Freshman
    • Age: 19.6
    • Height: 82 in
    • Weight: 250 lbs
    • Wingspan: 85 in
    • Ape: +3
    • ++: size, offensive creation, rim attacking
    • +: passing
    • -: defensive impact, shooting
    • Additional Notes: upside of a big wing who can be a primary offensive creator (arguably the most valuable archtype in the sport)

  2. Jabari Smith - PF - Auburn
    • Grade: Freshman
    • Age: 19.1
    • Height: 82 in
    • Weight: 210 lbs
    • ++: shooting
    • +: age, height, perimeter and on-ball defensive given size
    • -: ball handling, passing, offensive creation, interior defense
    • Additional Notes: probably won't be a primary creator, but his size+shooting is a great combination for a secondary threat, with defensive upside to boot

  3. Chet Holmgren - PF - Gonzaga
    • Grade: Freshman
    • Age: 20.1
    • Height: 84 in
    • Weight: 195 lbs
    • Wingspan: 89 in
    • Ape: +5
    • ++: rim protection, fluidity
    • +: shooting, off-ball abilities (both sides)
    • -: on-ball defense
    • --: age, frame
    • Additional Notes: for a player with such obvious strengths and weaknesses, it's difficult to determine what type of impact Holmgren will have given his uniqueness

  8. Shaedon Sharpe - SG - Kentucky
    • Grade: Freshman
    • Age: 19.1
    • Height: 77.25 in
    • Weight: 198 lbs
    • Wingspan: 83.5 in
    • Reach: 103.5 in
    • Ape: +6.25
    • ++: athleticism
    • +: age, frame, shooting, shot creation
    • --: production, unknowns
    • Additional Notes: at his best, he may have the highest offensive upside in the class, but the chances of reaching that upside are low enough and with a myriad of warts that he misses out on the 55 FV tier, while his monster upside keeps him above the 50 FV tier. Easily the most difficult player to rank from the outside.
