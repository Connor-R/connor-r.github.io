2021 NBA Draft Board

July 28, 2021

Introduction

I started late and am running short on time, so no intro this year. I think any intro-type information will be somehwere in my 2018, 2019, 2020, or 2016-2020 draft retrospective posts. Also, I was running out of time (please excuse any spelling/grammatical errors, I didn't do a lot of editing), so I only evaluated the top-40 players of an aggregating ranking I found of various sources. The players that made the aggregate list but I didn't evaluate are noted with whatever biographical data in Tier 8, ordered by their aggregate rank. Onto the tiers:

Tier 1 (~60 FV)

  1. Cade Cunningham - PG - Oklahoma State
    • Grade: Freshman
    • Age: 19.7
    • Height: 80 in
    • Weight: 220 lbs
    • ++: frame, shooting
    • +: on-ball defense
    • -: age, dribble penetration, playmaking, motor
    • Additional Notes: Shooting+switchable defensive profile creates highly scalable profile, a 'souped up 3&D' prospect, but may not excel if given the reigns as a lone shot creating alpha dog

  2. Evan Mobley - F-C - USC
    • Grade: Freshman
    • Age: 20
    • Height: 84 in
    • Weight: 215 lbs
    • ++: length, mobility at 7'0
    • +: rim protection while limiting fouls, scoring touch, playmaking
    • -: thin frame, may need to play next to a second big, shooting
    • --: age
    • Additional Notes: Is he a stretch-5 who can't shoot, or are his wing skills enough to make him a mismatch nightmare?
