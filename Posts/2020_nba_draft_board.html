2020 NBA Draft Board

November 9, 2020

Introduction

This is only as an exercise so I can look back in 5 years and tell myself how wrong my very basic gut instict combined with my idealized mental model of what is valuable in the NBA. In 2018 I spilled a lot of words and thoughts on the player archtypes, physical attributes, and skillsets that I do and do not value. In 2019 I talked about what I would be trying to maximize/optimize/solve for in terms of building a team/valuing prospects (tl;dr, a theoretically perfect version of how much a player can impact +/- if placed in their optimal role). This year, I'm not talking about anything because I'm not very good at this and just want to post this publicly so I can remind myself how not good at this I am. Onto the tiers:
